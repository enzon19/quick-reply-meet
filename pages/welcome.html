Welcome to

Quick Reply Meet

Add shortcuts to pre-defined messages made by you to send quickly in the chat.




Add your messages




1.
Click on the icon of the extension on the address bar.


2.
Create your own messages to send later on a meet.


3.
Go to a meet on Google Meet and use the buttons on the chat to send the messages.

Edit and delete




You can edit or delete the messages. The changes will be applied immediately.

Choose a set of messages

If you want, you can choose more than one set of messages to start using.


Set and personalize even more




You can personalize the time for the extension wait for append buttons on the chat and for check for the meet starts. This is good to adjust the extension even more for your computer. If your PC is slow, you can increase the time, and if your PC is fast, you can decrease.
